Family Assistant — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Family Assistant ("the Service") is a private, text-message-based personal assistant operated by an individual for use by members of their own household. This policy explains what information the Service handles and how it is treated.

Who this Service is for

The Service is private and non-commercial. Its only users are immediate family members of the operator who have personally opted in by texting the Service's phone number. The Service is not offered to the public and does not enroll outside recipients.

Information we collect

The Service processes the content of text messages sent to and from its phone number, along with the mobile phone numbers of family members who use it, in order to respond to requests and provide scheduling and reminders. With the family's permission, it may access the household's own Google Calendar and Gmail data solely to answer questions and create reminders for the family. This information is used only to operate the Service for the household.

Mobile information and sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information and messaging consent will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers, opt-in data, or any messaging information with any third parties. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are used only to operate the Service for the family and are not shared with anyone.

How we use your information

Mobile phone numbers and message content are used only to deliver the assistant's replies, daily summaries, and reminders that the family has requested. We do not use this information for advertising, marketing, or promotional purposes of any kind.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on family use. Recipients typically receive messages only in response to their own texts, plus at most one proactive daily summary message.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply, depending on the recipient's mobile carrier and plan.

Opting out

Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Replying HELP returns basic help information. These keywords are always honored.

Data retention

Message and reminder information is retained only as long as needed to operate the Service for the family, and may be deleted at any time at the operator's discretion.
