Family Assistant — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Family Assistant ("the Service") is a private, text-message-based personal assistant operated by an individual for use by members of their own household. This policy explains what information the Service handles and how it is treated.

Who this Service is for

The Service is private and non-commercial. Its only users are immediate family members of the operator who have personally opted in by texting the Service's phone number. The Service is not offered to the public and does not enroll outside recipients.

Information we handle

The Service processes the content of text messages sent to and from its phone number, along with the mobile phone numbers of family members who use it, in order to respond to requests and provide scheduling reminders. It may access the household's own calendar and email data, with permission, solely to answer questions and create reminders for the family.

Mobile information and sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers or any messaging information with third parties. Mobile opt-in data and phone numbers are used only to operate the Service for the family and are never shared for marketing or any other purpose.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on family use. Recipients typically receive messages only in response to their own texts, plus at most one proactive daily summary message.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply, depending on the recipient's mobile carrier and plan.

Opting out

Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Replying HELP returns basic help information.

Data retention

Message and reminder information is retained only as long as needed to operate the Service for the family, and may be deleted at any time at the operator's discretion.
